# Privacy Policy for Kings Corner Arena

**Last Updated: January 26, 2026**

## Introduction

Welcome to Kings Corner Arena ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you sign in using Sign in with Apple or other authentication providers, we may collect:
- Your unique user identifier
- Email address (if you choose to share it)
- Display name (if provided)
- Profile image URL

### Gameplay Data
We collect information about your gameplay to provide our services:
- Game scores and statistics
- Match history
- Daily challenge attempts and leaderboard rankings
- Win/loss records

### Device Information
We may automatically collect:
- Device type (iPhone, iPad)
- Operating system version
- App version
- General usage statistics

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide and maintain the game service
- Track your progress and statistics
- Display leaderboard rankings
- Improve game performance and features
- Communicate important updates about the service

## Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use PostgreSQL databases with encrypted connections to protect your information.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated statistics for analytical purposes.

## Third-Party Services

### Authentication Providers
When you use Sign in with Apple, Apple's privacy policy also applies to the data they process.

### Analytics
We may use analytics services to understand how our app is used. These services collect anonymized usage data.

## Children's Privacy

Kings Corner Arena is suitable for all ages (4+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request deletion of your account and data
- Opt out of optional data collection
- Export your gameplay data

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@kingscornerarena.com

## Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data within 30 days.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or via email.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us:

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what personal information we collect and the right to request deletion.

## European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have rights under GDPR including access, rectification, erasure, and data portability.
